Frequently Asked Questions

What is Life Insurance, and why do I need it?

Life insurance provides financial protection to your family in the event of your untimely demise. It ensures that your loved ones are financially secure even in your absence.

What are the types of Life Insurance policies available at LIC?

LIC offers a variety of plans including Term Insurance, Whole Life Insurance, Endowment Plans, Money Back Plans, and Pension/Retirement Plans. Each plan caters to different needs and life stages.

How can I check my LIC policy status online?

You can check your policy status by logging into the LIC online portal with your credentials. Alternatively, you can also check through the LIC mobile app or visit your nearest LIC office.

What happens if I miss paying my LIC premium?

If you miss paying your LIC premium, there is a grace period (usually 30 days) during which you can make the payment without any penalty. If you miss this period, your policy may lapse, but you can revive it under certain conditions.

Can I take a loan against my LIC policy?

Yes, you can avail a loan against certain LIC policies, such as Endowment and Money Back Plans, provided the policy has acquired a surrender value.

How can I claim the maturity or death benefits from LIC?

To claim maturity or death benefits, you can submit the necessary documents (policy bond, proof of death, etc.) to the LIC office. The settlement is typically processed within 30 days.
